Weird Music Guessing Game

by: Rachel Williams

This game was made for my level 2 project at The League of Amazing Programmers.
Click here to download and play!

1. Why I Made This Game

I decided to make this game because I always am listening to music and wanted to find to test other people on stupid songs.

2. What I Learned

I learned that I have an eclectic taste of music from user testing and that it was too hard.

3. Architecture of My Game

The different parts of my game are the 4 panels that interact with each other. Panel 1 is the main panel that holds the other 3 panels. The 2nd panel, which is the red one, hold the score, answer and question number. The 3rd one, the green one, has a button that plays a song. The 4th, blue panel, has a text field where the user puts their answer.

4. Technical Implementation

My game isn't very technical but I had to use an audio editing device to cut down the music files to the section of the song I wanted to play specifically.

5. Most Challenging Part

The most difficulties were technical issues that were hard to find in the code, for example having the final score showing up on the screen as there was a hidden panel that everything else was placed on.

6. Future Fixes and Updates

If I continued to work on this in the future I would make it so the player could chose from different levels of difficulty and types of music.
